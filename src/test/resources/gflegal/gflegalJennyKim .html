Jenny Kim practices general commercial real estate law and has handled a variety of transactions including purchase, sale, financing and leasing transactions of commercial property throughout the United States. Jenny has particular expertise in negotiating construction and mortgage loans for office, retail, multi-family and mixed use buildings and development sites in the New York metropolitan area, as well as other parts of the United States.

Representative transactions include the following:

  • $250,000,000 construction loan for the development of a mixed use building at the Brooklyn Navy Yard
  • $107,000,000 construction loan for the development of a co-location building on Roosevelt Island, NY
  • $550,000,000 refinancing of a Class A office building on Park Avenue in midtown Manhattan
  • $425,000,000 refinancing of a Class A office building in midtown Manhattan
  • $350,000,000 securitized mortgage and mezzanine loan financing of a retail shopping center in Westchester, NY
  • $65,000,000 mortgage loan of a net leased Class A office building in downtown Manhattan
  • $55,600,000 mortgage loan for the rehabilitation of a multifamily residential property in Los Angeles, CA
  • $95,000,000 construction loan for the rehabilitation of a multifamily residential building in uptown Manhattan
  • $52,000,000 mortgage loan for the acquisition of a multifamily residential property in Los Angeles, CA
  • $31,700,000 mortgage loan for a portfolio of 7 multifamily residential buildings in Manhattan

Prior to joining Goldfarb & Fleece LLP, Jenny was an associate in the real estate group at Simpson Thacher & Bartlett LLP.
